# Payment Gateway Options in Cameroon

Accepting digital payments is essential for modern businesses in Cameroon. This guide covers your options for setting up payment gateways.

## Mobile Money Gateways

### MTN Mobile Money

MTN Mobile Money is one of the most popular payment methods in Cameroon.

**Requirements:**
- Valid RCCM registration
- Taxpayer card (NIU)
- Business bank account
- Valid business address
- Manager's national ID

**Application Process:**
1. Visit an MTN Mobile Money agent or service center
2. Submit your business documents
3. Complete the merchant application form
4. Wait for approval (typically 5-10 business days)
5. Receive your merchant account details

**Fees:**
- Setup fee: Varies by business type
- Transaction fees: Typically 1-2% per transaction
- Monthly maintenance: May apply for some account types

### Orange Money

Orange Money offers similar services with competitive rates.

**Requirements:**
- Same as MTN Mobile Money
- Business must be registered and operational

**Key Differences:**
- Different fee structure
- Separate application process
- May have different transaction limits

**Tip:** Consider applying for both to give customers more payment options.

## Bank Payment Gateways

Many banks in Cameroon offer payment gateway services for online businesses.

### Popular Bank Options

- **Afriland First Bank**: Offers merchant services
- **UBA Cameroon**: Payment gateway solutions
- **Ecobank**: Digital payment integration
- **Société Générale**: Business payment solutions

### What You'll Need

- Business bank account (already opened)
- RCCM extract
- Taxpayer card
- Business website or online presence (for online gateways)
- Technical documentation (for API integration)

## Choosing the Right Solution

### For Physical Stores

- **Mobile Money POS**: Accept payments via QR codes or USSD
- **Bank POS terminals**: Traditional card payment machines
- **Hybrid solutions**: Accept both mobile money and cards

### For Online Businesses

- **Payment gateway APIs**: Integrate with your website
- **Payment links**: Send payment requests via SMS or email
- **E-commerce plugins**: Use pre-built solutions

## Application Timeline

**Mobile Money:**
- Application: 1-2 days
- Review: 3-5 business days
- Approval and setup: 1-2 days
- **Total: 5-10 business days**

**Bank Gateways:**
- Application: 2-3 days
- Review: 5-7 business days
- Technical setup: 3-5 business days
- **Total: 10-15 business days**

## Common Challenges

1. **Documentation delays**: Ensure all documents are current and complete
2. **Technical requirements**: Online gateways may need developer assistance
3. **Approval process**: Can take longer during peak periods
4. **Fee structures**: Compare fees across providers

## Best Practices

- **Start early**: Apply before you need the service
- **Keep documents ready**: Maintain updated business documents
- **Compare options**: Research fees and features
- **Test thoroughly**: Test payment flows before going live
- **Monitor transactions**: Set up alerts for all transactions
- **Customer support**: Choose providers with good support

## Security Considerations

- Use secure payment methods
- Never share merchant credentials
- Monitor for suspicious activity
- Keep software updated
- Use HTTPS for online payments
- Comply with PCI DSS if handling card data

## Integration Tips

### For Developers

- Use official APIs and SDKs
- Implement proper error handling
- Test in sandbox environments first
- Handle webhooks securely
- Log all transactions

### For Non-Technical Users

- Use payment link services
- Consider e-commerce platforms with built-in gateways
- Work with a developer or agency
- Use official mobile apps from providers

## Next Steps

After setting up your payment gateway:

1. Test with small transactions
2. Train your staff on the system
3. Display payment options to customers
4. Monitor transaction reports regularly
5. Keep backup payment methods available
