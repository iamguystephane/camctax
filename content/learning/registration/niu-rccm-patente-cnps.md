# Overview

Understanding business paperwork in Cameroon is the first step to compliance.

## Key documents

- NIU: taxpayer identification number used in all tax interactions.
- RCCM: commercial registry number proving legal existence.
- Patente: municipal business license paid annually.
- CNPS: social security registration for employers and workers.

## What to do next

- Gather IDs and any prior receipts.
- Decide your business form (ETS, SARL, SA).
- Keep simple monthly records of sales and expenses.
